Quick Answers

Frequently Asked
Questions

Can't find an answer? Our curators are a message away.

How quickly will I receive a response?

Our team typically responds within 24 hours on business days. For urgent enquiries, WhatsApp is the fastest way to reach us directly.

Do you only cover Sri Lanka?

Sri Lanka is our speciality, but we also arrange bespoke regional tours that may include the Maldives or southern India as natural extensions to your journey.

Can I fully customise my itinerary?

Absolutely. Every journey we create is tailored to your interests, pace, and budget. We use signature journeys only as creative inspiration — never templates.

What is your cancellation policy?

Policies vary by supplier and season. We outline full terms before any commitment is made, and we always recommend comprehensive travel insurance for every journey.

Do you arrange visa support?

We provide full guidance on Sri Lanka's electronic travel authorisation (ETA) process and can liaise with your accommodation for invitation letters where required.

Is there a minimum budget for a trip?

We work best with travellers looking for curated, mid-to-luxury experiences. Journeys typically start from around $2,000 per person, depending on duration and style.
