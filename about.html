Our Story

A journey that began
on a rainy afternoon in Ella.

In 2016, Rohan and Priya — two old friends with careers in hospitality and photography — found themselves sharing a cup of Ceylon tea, watching mist roll over the Nine Arch Bridge. The travellers around them had itineraries, but they didn't have experiences.

That conversation became a plan, and that plan became TravelSeek. A company built on the conviction that Sri Lanka is not a destination to be ticked off a list — it's a feeling, a layered, living world that takes time, local knowledge, and genuine curiosity to truly understand.

Today, every journey we design carries that original spirit: unhurried, personal, and deeply rooted in the places we love.

Vision & Mission

Our Vision

To See the World Differently

We envision a world where travel is a force for connection — between people, cultures, and the natural world. TravelSeek aspires to be the most trusted name in experiential travel across South Asia, setting the standard for journeys that are as meaningful as they are memorable.

Travel that enriches both traveller and destination
Authentic encounters beyond the tourist trail

Our Mission

To Move You, Truly

Our mission is to craft bespoke Sri Lankan journeys that transcend sightseeing — creating genuine emotional connections between our guests and the island's landscapes, people, and culture. We do this through meticulous curation, deep local relationships, and an unwavering commitment to excellence at every step.

Every itinerary handcrafted, never templated
Local guides, not tour operators — community first

Meet the Founders

Two friends united by a shared love for this extraordinary island and a desire to share it the right way.

Rohan Fernando

Co-Founder & Chief Experience Officer

With over 15 years in luxury hospitality across Asia, Rohan brings an encyclopaedic knowledge of Sri Lanka's hidden corners — from the mist-wrapped estates of Nuwara Eliya to the ancient temples of the Cultural Triangle.

Priya Mendis

Co-Founder & Creative Director

A former travel photographer and visual storyteller, Priya spent a decade documenting communities across Southeast Asia before co-founding TravelSeek. Her lens trained her to see what most visitors miss — the quiet details, the unrepeatable moments, the soul of a place.
