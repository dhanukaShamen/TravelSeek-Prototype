Your Itinerary

Day 1–2

Arrival & The Wallawwa

Arrive in Colombo. Private transfer to The Wallawwa, a restored 18th-century manor house. Unwind in the tropical gardens before your journey begins.

Day 3–4

Kandy & The Temple of the Tooth

Travel to the royal city of Kandy. Visit the sacred Temple of the Tooth Relic at sunset. Stay at The Kandy House, enjoying authentic Kandyan cuisine.

Day 5–7

The Scenic Train & Nuwara Eliya

Board the blue train for one of the world's most scenic rail journeys. Arrive in 'Little England'. High tea at the Grand Hotel and a private tour of a working tea factory.

Day 8–10

Ella & Departure

Hike to Little Adam's Peak for sunrise. Relax in a boutique mountain retreat. Private transfer back to Colombo for your departure flight.
