Central Province

Ella & The Highlands

Where the clouds descend to touch the tea leaves.

Nestled in a valley surrounded by cloud forests and tea plantations, Ella is the heart of Sri Lanka's hill country. It is a place where time slows down, governed only by the rolling mists and the distant whistle of the train.

Beyond the famous Nine Arch Bridge, we guide you to hidden waterfalls and private estates where the legacy of Ceylon Tea is preserved in every cup.

Best Time to Visit

January to March is driest. August offers pleasant breezes.

Climate

Cool & Temperate. Average 20°C (68°F).

Getting There

6-hour scenic train from Kandy or 5-hour drive from Colombo.
